A library system is the infrastructure and processes that manage a library's resources and services. It includes cataloging and classification, acquisition and collection development, circulation, reference and information services, digital libraries and online resources, interlibrary loan, management and administration, and technology infrastructure. The goal is to efficiently organize and provide access to information to meet the needs of library users.
